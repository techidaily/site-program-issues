### Fix 4 – Turn off overlays

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/2-2.jpg)

**2)** Click**Video** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/12/4-2.jpg)

**4)** Set Fullscreen Mode to**Windowed** or**Borderless** .
